    About

    Learn More About Us

    We are a community of pottery makers in Romania that love to work with clay and create unique peaces that could make your dinners look stunning .

    Our experience in creating tableware pottery is longstanding and the love for the craft has inspired us to create high quality pieces

    • Working with high quality porcelain
    • Experimenting with techniques gives us the chance to create unique pieces
    • Our producers are artists in their craft

    We have been on the market since 2012 with a total of more than 12000 unique pieces of pottery sold and distributed to happy clients. We envisage a line of tableware were not one piece is like the other but still maintains the etiquete of the royal houses of the world
